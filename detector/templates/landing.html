Welcome!

Welcome to IncepTB, a PA CXR analysis tool for detection of Tuberculosis.

About

IncepTB is an AI-powered tool designed for fast and accessible screening of Tuberculosis using chest X-ray images. It leverages CNNs and XAI for visual explanations to assist healthcare professionals.

Instructions

  • Upload a chest X-ray image (in .png format) using the Upload button on the side panel.
  • Receive an AI prediction of the diagnosis.
  • View visual explanation via Grad-CAM.

FAQ

What images are accepted? Only PA chest X-ray images in .png format.

Can I upload a DICOM file? No. DICOM files are not accepted. Please convert your chest X-ray to .png format before uploading.

What model does IncepTB use? IncepTB uses the InceptionV3 deep learning model, fine-tuned for Tuberculosis detection.

How is the confidence score computed? The system provides a confidence score (probability) for both TB-Positive and TB-Negative results. This reflects the model's certainty in its prediction.

How accurate is the prediction? The model is trained on validated datasets and a healthcare professional will decide the final interpretation.

Disclaimer

This tool is not a replacement for medical diagnosis. Results should be confirmed by a healthcare professional.
